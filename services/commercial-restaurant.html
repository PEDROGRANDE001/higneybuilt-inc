Commercial Restaurant Build-Outs

From Bare Shell to Opening Night

A restaurant is the hardest commercial space there is to build, three inspectors, a dozen trades, and a hard opening date that never moves. HigneyBuilt builds them start to finish: dining rooms, bars, patios, storefronts, and the back-of-house that feeds them, delivered ready to serve.

Request a Consultation Call (310) 463-4922

We Speak Restaurant

Nobody feels a blown schedule like a restaurant owner. Every day past the opening date is rent paid, staff hired, and revenue lost on a room that cannot serve a single cover. That pressure is exactly why restaurants need a builder who has done it before, one who knows how the health department, the fire marshal, and the building inspector each read the same plan differently.

We build the entire box. Dining rooms with the finishes and lighting that set the mood, bars and service stations, exposed structural ceilings, custom millwork and slat walls, storefronts and roll-up windows, covered patios, and the ADA restrooms and paths that have to be right the first time. Behind it all we run the mechanical, electrical, and plumbing that a commercial kitchen demands, sequenced so the finishes go in clean and last.

From the raw shell to the plated first service, we coordinate every trade, your designer, and the city so the doors open on the day you promised your investors they would.

  • Full restaurant build-outs from bare shell to opening
  • Dining rooms, bars, and service stations
  • Exposed structural ceilings, millwork, and feature walls
  • Storefronts, pass-through windows, and covered patios
  • Commercial kitchens, MEP, and grease-duct coordination
  • ADA compliance, health, fire, and building inspection sign-off
