Commercial Kitchen Build-Outs

Where the Real Work Happens

A restaurant lives or dies on its back-of-house. HigneyBuilt builds commercial kitchens that pass inspection, run clean, and hold up to a full service every night, hoods and fire suppression, walk-ins, stainless, gas, plumbing, and finishes built to health-code and ready to cook.

Request a Consultation Call (310) 463-4922

Built to Pass, Built to Cook

A commercial kitchen is the most demanding room in any build. It has to satisfy the health department, the fire marshal, and the building inspector, and then it has to survive a line cook slamming it for twelve hours a day. There is no room for shortcuts, and no hiding a bad detail once the equipment is set.

We build the whole box: Type I exhaust hoods and fire suppression, make-up air, grease-duct routing, walk-in coolers and freezers, floor drains and grease interceptors, stainless wall and ceiling systems, gas lines, and the electrical and plumbing rough-in that ties it all together. Every trade is sequenced so the equipment sets clean and the finishes go in last, sealed, sloped to drain, and ready for the health inspector.

From a single hood swap to a full restaurant back-of-house from bare shell, we coordinate with your kitchen equipment supplier, your engineers, and the city so it opens on time and passes the first walkthrough.

  • Type I & Type II exhaust hoods, make-up air, and fire suppression
  • Walk-in coolers, freezers, and refrigeration infrastructure
  • Grease interceptors, floor sinks, and sloped drainage
  • Stainless wall and ceiling cladding to health-code finish
  • Gas, plumbing, and electrical rough-in for the full cook line
  • Equipment coordination, inspection, and health-department sign-off
